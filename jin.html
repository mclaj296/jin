JIN

Jin is a tattoo artist based out of Seoul and Daejeon Korea. They have a very unique style of tattooing using bold colors with a rubber hose style of drawing. They primarily tattoo wacky pop culture references or drawings of their own style. They can be found on instagram @3.jinnn
